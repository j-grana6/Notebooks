While some of the $0$ values might be because the employment is actually $0$ (or close to $0$) that is certainly not the case for all zipcodes. For example, take a look at the time series of zipcode 20010 (where I used to live in DC).

def plot_employment_at_zip(zipcode, data=rawdata):
    zipdata = data[data["zip"]==zipcode]
    ax = zipdata.plot("year", "emp", legend=True, label="Employment in zipcode " +str(zipcode))
    #plt.legend("Employment")
    return ax
